About Kumano Translation

We are based right on the edge of the Kumano Kodo world heritage area – a region covered in thick forest with a sunny climate.

Twenty-five years of experience

We were sending translated documents via modem before the internet. A Mac used to cost 10,000 dollars.

Services

If it is in japanese, we can translate it. Our rates are very reasonable, The prices are upfront and we will not send you a translation without proofreading it and then surprise you with extra fees for proofreading. Layout does cost x per page on top of translation, however.

This being said, we often can not proofread Japanese-to-Emglish translations done by Indian and Japanese translators for the usual rate, as they usually need significant work. Let us know if you adhere to a certain style guide, and if you do not that is OK - just specify American or British English.

Native English
