Services

If it is in japanese, we can translate it. Our rates are very reasonable, The prices are upfront and we will not send you a translation without proofreading it and then surprise you with extra fees for proofreading. Layout does cost x per page on top of translation, however..

This being said, we often can not proofread Japanese-to-Emglish translations done by Indian and Japanese translators for the usual rate, as they usually need significant work.

Let us know if you adhere to a certain style guide, and if you do not that is OK - just specify American or British English.

As much as we would like to hire other translators, over the years we have found almost none with the necessary level of expertise and attention to detail. So, unlike others who provide translation services in dozens of languages for the price of a bowl of curry, we only do what we are good at and we charge accordingly. Please call or email for a quote.
